Meet Our Founder

Mitchell Brouwer founded Brouwer Digital with a focus on building digital products that solve real problems. Based in Australia, we work with clients to create custom applications while also developing our own innovative products.

We combine technical expertise with business understanding, delivering full-stack solutions from web applications to mobile apps. Our approach emphasizes AI integration where it genuinely improves efficiency, not just for the sake of using AI.

Whether building custom solutions for clients or creating our own products like educational AR games, we focus on delivering real value through thoughtful technology choices and clean implementation.
